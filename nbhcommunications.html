The Project

Tooskee is an early-stage startup aiming to revolutionize food management. Although mobile technology has reshaped nearly every aspect of our daily lives, we've yet to digitize the kitchen. We make too much pasta; we toss away the leftovers. Waste entire loafs of bread. And we rely on take-out meals that are not part of a sound meal planning system - not to mention a healthy diet. All told, an average family of four in America loses $1600 to food waste. Enter: Tooskee! Tooskee is your smart home food management system. By managing food inventory and offering ways to improve habits, it transforms your kitchen into an environmentally sustainable and cost-effective culinary space!
