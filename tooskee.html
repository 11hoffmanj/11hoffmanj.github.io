Tooskee

DIGITIZE YOUR KITCHEN.

The Scoop

Tooskee is an early-stage startup aiming to revolutionize food management. Although mobile technology has reshaped nearly every aspect of our daily lives, we've yet to digitize the kitchen. We make too much pasta; we toss away the leftovers. Waste entire loafs of bread. And we rely on take-out meals that are not part of a sound meal planning system - not to mention a healthy diet. All told, an average family of four in America loses $1600 to food waste. Enter: Tooskee! Tooskee is your smart home food management system. By managing food inventory and offering ways to improve habits, it transforms your kitchen into an environmentally sustainable and cost-effective culinary space!

Professionally yummy

Simplicity All Over

An efficient and user-friendly kitchen database is perfect for young, tech-savvy professionals. For Tooskee's strawberry, I set out to marry motifs of techie innovation with comfortable, familial food imagery. I decided to riff on flat design to give it a modern, simple look.

Scrumptiously Modern

Tooskee's aesthetic makes judicious use of white space, fruity colors, thin typefaces, and pull-out navigation. I banked on swiping gestures playing a large role in the organization of the "virtual kitchen" rather than more traditional menu based interfaces. In many ways the design of this App was informed by the stylistic choices of iOS 7.

Trading Cards

We were recently invited to take part in the Thought for Food Summit in Berlin, in September 2013. We networked with some of the top activists and shakers in the world of food security -- from the scientist who has grown the first in vitro meat to vertical farming enthusiasts. All were supportive of Tooskee and its mission: to eliminate food waste by creating value for every day folks.
