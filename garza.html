The Project

Garza is a Wordpress theme designed for a friend of mine, Maria, who is a model and rock climbing enthusiast from Venezuela. She's travelled all over the world for modeling, and wanted a blog to document her adventures. I discussed much with Maria -- about thin lines, light colors, and more -- and this motivated many choices that went into the final product. When it was all said and done, Garza came out to be a feminine, vivacious and youthful design. Take a look!

Lovely Lights

Color scheme sets the tone for design. Garza is feminine, and it starts with the colors. The three main colors are a lightened triad of primaries: pink, light blue, and yellow. The resultant? A light and lovely scheme that establishes a feeling of vibrance, elegance, and youth.

Sharp Typography

For a font, I wanted something delicate, readable, and still funky. Rawengulk turned up; with its thin lines and tight serif indications, it perfectly mirrored the cutting strength of a climber and the elegance of a model.
